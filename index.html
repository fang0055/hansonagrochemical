MISSION

Founded in 2014, the mission of Hanson Agrochemical Consulting is to provide customers with the quality services on the innovation, registration, promotion, and market penetration of agrochemical products, thus bridging the product supplies to the end-users.

VISION

Our vision is to bring the farmers in North America with an alternative, competitive, feasible, and fordable tool, such as generic herbicides, to tackle crop protection issues, with an aim to boom and robust agricultural industries.

WHO ARE WE

Hanson Agrochemical Consulting is an affiliate of Hanson Industry Company, located in Stittsville, Ontario, Canada. We have been registering agrochemicals in PMRA and CFIA, carrying out product market surveys, recruiting the new pesticide compounds for years. Our team is composed of the registration professionals, registration specialists, marketing professionals, and experienced salesmen.

WHAT WE DO

At Hanson, our number one specialization is pesticide registration. There are many steps and hurdles in the way to register your product, but we've got you covered.
