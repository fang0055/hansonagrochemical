Hanson Helps Chinese Pesticide Manufacturers Register Four AIs in PMRA

Since 2015, Hanson Industry Company has been a leader in bridging the Canadian crop protection market needs with Chinese Pesticide suppliers. We have successfully aided multiple Chinese pesticide manufacturers to register 4 technical products, which include glufosinate, florasulam, boscalid and s-metolachlor at PMRA, Health Canada. The registration certificate of glufosinate 200 g/L has been issued at PMRA. More generic active ingredients have been, and will be submitted to PMRA soon, which include formulations of glyphosate, glufosinate, clodinafop-propargyl 2,4-D, clethodim, pyraclostrobin and others. We also aid in the registration and commercialization of other products, such as plant growth regulators (PGRs), biostimulants, bio-pesticides, beneficial insects.

Agrow China Top 30 Companies 2016

his is the first comprehensive China Top 30 Crop Protection Companies report, derived from primary market research throughout the regions and provinces. It is copiously illustrated throughout with totally original data. Given the current interest in China’s growing clout in the global crop protection sector, the authors believe there is further scope for both consolidation and diversification, both in the domestic market and beyond. The Chinese crop protection market is valued at US$ 5.2 billion at provincial distributor level, making it the third largest in the world. Around two-thirds of the market is generic, and domestic products account for 65%, while imported products account for 35%. The authors examine each major Chinese CP company in detail - their history and evolution; products; sales; manufacturing capabilities; market positioning and key drivers; and R&D pipeline strategies.
